Get Involved

rOpenSci is a collaborative effort to develop R-based tools for facilitating Open Science. Learn more and follow us on twitter and Google+ for updates.

There are many ways to get involved with the rOpenSci project.

  1. Keep up with latest updates on Twitter.
  2. Link to us from your blog.
  3. Collaborate with us on the different packages via github.
  4. Get in touch if you would like to join our development team.

We are always open to new ideas and collaborations. For faster contact, please email info at this domain name.
